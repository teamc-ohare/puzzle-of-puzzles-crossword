How to Play

Welcome to Puzzle of Puzzles, a tier'd crossword puzzle game that's based around the O'Hare International Airport! When you click Start Game from the game page, you'll be directed to choose a difficulty level. There are four difficulties to choose: Easy, Intermediate, Normal & Advanced--with a chance to unlock a hidden crossword puzzle. Each level of difficulty contains five word puzzles. Once a level of difficulty is selected, you'll receive a random word puzzle from that tier. Each word puzzle completed will display an image, based upon the O'Hare International Airport object the puzzle is based upon. When you've completed a word puzzle, you can either Continue or Exit. Continueing will generate a new word puzzle from the selected tier, Exiting will return you to the main page. If you complete all five puzzles of a selected difficulty, then you've completed the level, at which time you'll be asked to proceed to the next level or exit the game. How to unlock the hidden puzzle won't be explained, but rest assure, if you're paying attention, you'll catch the clue. In order to view the entire storyline of the game, and WIN the game, the hidden puzzle must be unlocked and completed. If you WIN the game, by completing the hidden word puzzle, you can opt-in at the end of the game to be included in the FEATURED list by entering your name and where you're from.

Tips for Gameplay

  • BLACK tiles cannot hold letters.
  • Unanswered/Incorrectly answered tiles remain WHITE.
  • Clicking into a WHITE tile, turns the tile YELLOW, opening the CURSOR.
  • Correctly answered tiles are GREEN.
  • Grid tiles convert all letters to UPPERCASE.
  • This is a THEMED puzzle, all Across & Down Lists and their Answers are based around the O'Hare International Airport, including Aviation of Things.
  • The challenge can be completed on personal devices, at lounges, inflight, at home, etc.
  • Remember, there is a chance to unlock a hidden puzzle, and be FEATURED, PAY ATTENTION FOR CLUES!

When you're ready for the challenge, click the button below to start your difficulty.
