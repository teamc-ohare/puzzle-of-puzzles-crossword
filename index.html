How to Play

Welcome to the O'Hare Crossword Puzzle. To start, click the Start Game button in navaigation panel on the left side of the page. When you click Start Game, you'll be directed to choose a difficulty level. Currently, there in one difficulty to choose: Normal. All crossword puzzles are based upon the O'Hare International Airport. Once a level of difficulty is selected, you'll receive a random word puzzle from that tier. Each word puzzle completed will display an image, based upon the O'Hare International Airport object the puzzle is based upon. If you COMPLETE the word puzzle, you'll be directed to contant form where you can enter your name and home town, to be displayed in the FEATURED list, showing you've completed the puzzle. If you do not wish to be featured, you can simply exit the game.

When you're ready for the challenge, click the button below to start your difficulty.
