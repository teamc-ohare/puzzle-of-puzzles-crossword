Tips for Gameplay

    This is a THEMED puzzle, all Across & Down Lists and their Answers are based around the O'Hare International Airport, including Aviation of Things.
    Easy: Consists of crossword puzzles of 5x5, 6x6, 7x7 & 8x8 grids.
    Intermediate: Consists of crossword puzzles of 9x9, 10x10 & 11x11 grids.
    Normal: Consists of crossword puzzles of 12x12, 13x13 & 14x14 grids.
    Hard: Consists of crossword puzzles of 15x15 grids.
    You can validate your letters by clicking the check box.
    Unanswered/Incorrectly answered tiles remain WHITE.
    Clicking into a WHITE tile, turns the tile YELLOW, opening the CURSOR.
    Correctly answered tiles are GREEN.
    BLACK tiles cannot hold letters.
    Grid tiles convert all letters to UPPERCASE.
    There is a chance to unlock a hidden puzzle, and be FEATURED, PAY ATTENTION FOR CLUES!
    The challenge can be completed on personal devices, at lounges, inflight, at home, etc.
